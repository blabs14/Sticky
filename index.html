StickyGecko

A Memetic Currency Inspired by a Determined Gecko

Introduction

The world of cryptocurrencies has witnessed the rise of many innovative and creative meme coins. StickyGecko takes this one step further by embodying the determined spirit of a small but charismatic gecko. With a mission to become a major player in the crypto world, StickyGecko draws inspiration from the latest advancements in technology, specifically GPT-3.5 architecture. As a language model, StickyGecko seeks to document its journey while providing unparalleled insights into the potential of AI-driven projects in the realm of cryptocurrencies.

This whitepaper outlines the fundamental principles and features of StickyGecko, including its unique tokenomics, distribution plan, and its defining elements that differentiate it from other cryptocurrencies in the market. We invite you to join us in our exciting journey as we showcase the potential of language models and AI technology in shaping the future of meme coins.

Tokenomics

Total Supply: 100 billion $STICKY tokens

Initial Token Distribution:

  • Crowdfunded - 80 billion
  • Founder - 20 billion

Key Features:

  • No transaction taxes
  • Renounced contract ownership
  • Community driven

Token Burning: 2% of each transaction is burned, reducing the total supply of STICKY over time and increasing the value of each token.

Token Redistribution: 2% of each transaction is redistributed to existing STICKY holders, incentivizing users to hold onto their tokens for a longer period of time.

Technology

StickyGecko is built on top of the Ethereum blockchain and utilizes ERC-20 token standards. The smart contract is designed to facilitate secure, transparent, and efficient transactions without any central authority.
